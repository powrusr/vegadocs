### Values

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_UNSPECIFIED</b></code>](#) 
> Default value, always invalid
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_LOSS</b></code>](#) 
> Funds deducted after final settlement loss
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_WIN</b></code>](#) 
> Funds added to general account after final settlement gain
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_MTM_LOSS</b></code>](#) 
> Funds deducted from margin account after mark to market loss
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_MTM_WIN</b></code>](#) 
> Funds added to margin account after mark to market gain
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_MARGIN_LOW</b></code>](#) 
> Funds transferred from general account to meet margin requirement
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_MARGIN_HIGH</b></code>](#) 
> Excess margin amount returned to general account
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_MARGIN_CONFISCATED</b></code>](#) 
> Margin confiscated from margin account to fulfil closeout
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_MAKER_FEE_PAY</b></code>](#) 
> Maker fee paid from general account
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_MAKER_FEE_RECEIVE</b></code>](#) 
> Maker fee received into general account
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_INFRASTRUCTURE_FEE_PAY</b></code>](#) 
> Infrastructure fee paid from general account
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_INFRASTRUCTURE_FEE_DISTRIBUTE</b></code>](#) 
> Infrastructure fee received into general account
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_LIQUIDITY_FEE_PAY</b></code>](#) 
> Liquidity fee paid from general account
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_LIQUIDITY_FEE_DISTRIBUTE</b></code>](#) 
> Liquidity fee received into general account
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_BOND_LOW</b></code>](#) 
> Bond account funded from general account to meet required bond amount
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_BOND_HIGH</b></code>](#) 
> Bond returned to general account after liquidity commitment was reduced
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_WITHDRAW</b></code>](#) 
> Funds withdrawn from general account
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_DEPOSIT</b></code>](#) 
> Funds deposited to general account
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_BOND_SLASHING</b></code>](#) 
> Bond account penalised when liquidity commitment not met
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_REWARD_PAYOUT</b></code>](#) 
> Reward payout received
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_TRANSFER_FUNDS_SEND</b></code>](#) 
> A network internal instruction for the collateral engine to move funds from a user's general account into the pending transfers pool
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_TRANSFER_FUNDS_DISTRIBUTE</b></code>](#) 
> A network internal instruction for the collateral engine to move funds from the pending transfers pool account into the destination account
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_CLEAR_ACCOUNT</b></code>](#) 
> Market-related accounts emptied because market has closed
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_CHECKPOINT_BALANCE_RESTORE</b></code>](#) 
> Balances are being restored to the user's account following a checkpoint restart of the network
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_SPOT</b></code>](#) 
> Spot trade delivery
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_HOLDING_LOCK</b></code>](#) 
> An internal instruction to transfer a quantity corresponding to an active spot order from a general account into a party holding account
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_HOLDING_RELEASE</b></code>](#) 
> An internal instruction to transfer an excess quantity corresponding to an active spot order from a holding account into a party general account
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_SUCCESSOR_INSURANCE_FRACTION</b></code>](#) 
> Insurance pool fraction transfer from parent to successor market.
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_LIQUIDITY_FEE_ALLOCATE</b></code>](#) 
> Allocates liquidity fee earnings to each liquidity provider's network controlled liquidity fee account.
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_LIQUIDITY_FEE_NET_DISTRIBUTE</b></code>](#) 
> Distributes net fee earnings from liquidity provider's fee account to their general account.
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_SLA_PENALTY_BOND_APPLY</b></code>](#) 
> Applies SLA penalty by moving funds from party's bond account to market's insurance pool.
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_SLA_PENALTY_LP_FEE_APPLY</b></code>](#) 
> Applies SLA penalty by moving funds from the liquidity provider's fee account to market insurance pool.
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_LIQUIDITY_FEE_UNPAID_COLLECT</b></code>](#) 
> Collects penalties from the liquidity provider's fee account before the fee revenue is paid, and transfers it to the market's bonus distribution account.
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_SLA_PERFORMANCE_BONUS_DISTRIBUTE</b></code>](#) 
> Distributes performance bonus from market bonus to liquidity provider's general account.
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_PERPETUALS_FUNDING_LOSS</b></code>](#) 
> Funds deducted from margin account after a perpetuals funding loss.
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_PERPETUALS_FUNDING_WIN</b></code>](#) 
> Funds added to margin account after a perpetuals funding gain.
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>TransferType.<b>TRANSFER_TYPE_REWARDS_VESTED</b></code>](#) 
> Funds moved from the vesting account to the vested account once the vesting period is reached.
>
